Certified Transparency — your reliable ownership service

Who wrote it first?

Ever wondered who wrote your favorite song first? Or if someone is claiming your work as their own?
Certified Transparency is here to the rescue - we certify the ownership of any digital goods, for free! Join the Certified Transparency Community now!

1. Ownership Certificates

We record your ownership of almost anything. You decide if it shall be announced or not.

2. Monitor Ownerships

Everyone in the CT Community can follow the newest ownerships, by monitoring our announcements.

3. Claim Infringements

Found an offending ownership in our announcements? Use your certificate to file a claim! We'll conn ect you so that you can sort this out.

Why trust you?

Our protocol and software are open source. So is all our data. Everyone can check our operations with cryptographic proofs powered by our Merkle Hash Tree. No one will ever be able to alter any recorded data!

We never store the data you own, and we protect your provided contact information with state-of-the-art encryption.

Get started!

Get yourself a copy of the Certified Transparency Client application.

# general syntax:
bin/client [--server="1.2.3.4"] <command>

bin/client --help  # overview

bin/client preregister --name "John" /your/file  # => you get a non-public certificate
bin/client register --name "John" --data-private "Sorry, wasn't aware" --data-public 'please write infringement@you.com' /your/file  # => you get a non-public certificate

bin/client monitor  # keep an eye on other's ownerships
bin/client claim "data-client/sot/123.sot" 456  # claim your ownership on submitted ownership #456
